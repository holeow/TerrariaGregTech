A full port of GregTech Modern (GTCEu). Up stands from "Unofficially ported"
We want to greg everywhere

> Note: This is an unofficial port. Do not request support in original mod

Adds:
- ~8700 new items
- ~32000 recipes
- 70 singleblock machines, 15 voltage tiers
- 55 multiblocks
- Wire, item, fluid, laser, optical and long-distance pipe networks
- Cover system
- Gregtech ore generation and compatibility with terraria ores
- Various recipe changes for proper integration into terraria progression
- JEI-like recipe browser
- Questbook (ported from GTCEu Community Edition, not polished yet)
- Applied Energistics 2 (autocrafting and terminals)
- Full multiplayer compatibility
- Magic Storage compatibility with item pipes and ae2 cables

(Recipe Browser and AE2 will become separate mods eventually)

Discord: https://discord.gg/sqs4G7u6eX
